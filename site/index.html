Edinburgh Hacklab

Edinburgh Hacklab is part of the worldwide movement of Hackerspaces - Shared spaces for people who mess around with technology for fun.

By creating a shared space to do this, we provide access to better facilities than we could each have at home, as well as opportunities to collaborate, learn, and socialise. We are not a coworking space.

For more info on what our members are up to, check our blog.

How to become a member

You don’t need to be a member to come along to our open nights, but members get:

  • 24/7 access to the lab
  • Storage space for their projects
  • Cheaper rates on some tools
  • Warm, fuzzy feelings for supporting an awesome place

Ask any member for more information.

Come & Visit

We're open to the public every Tuesday, 7PM till late.

If you can’t make Tuesday, then any member can accompany you and show you around - just ask through any of our chat platforms.
