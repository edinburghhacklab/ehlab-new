By becoming a member you help support the lab and ensure the continued existence. In return you get some great benefits of membership including:

  • 24/7 access to the space.
  • Use of the lab equipment that other members have donated and allowed to be used communally.
  • Space to leave your own project work at the lab.
  • Opportunity to get involved in the organisation of the lab including voting and standing in Directorship elections

Membership starts at £35/month with the option to pay more if you like. If you would like to become a member, but £35/month is a barrier please discuss it with us at an open night or contact us, circumstances will be considered.

Before becoming a member it's important for us to get to know you a bit (and vice versa). The best way to this is to come to a few open nights. It's also a great opportunity to ask any questions you might have about membership or the hacklab in general.
