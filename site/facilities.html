The Hacklab is spread across 5 rooms – G1, G2, G8, G11, G14. Equipment moves around from time to time, so ask a member about a particular piece of kit. This page is not updated as frequently as the wiki, so for more up to date info see there.

Main Lab – G1

The Main Lab is the main social area where most activity takes place, whether a group of people working on a software project or somebody working on some electronics. The soothing ambient tunes of Internet radio SomaFM or more upbeat Scenesat often play from the Hi-Fi.

It also houses the electronics corner, including:

  • Soldering iron & hot air station
  • Binocular microscope – Ideal for soldering small projects
  • Oscilloscopes. Lots of ’em, from Tek boat-anchors to a shiny Rigol digital ‘scope.
  • Bench Power supplies
  • Signal generator
  • Lots of resistors, capacitors, and other standard components
  • Every type of cable, convertor and adapter imaginable (and a few unimaginable….)

And the radio corner. We have these antennas mounted on the roof:

  • Active mini-whip antenna
  • VHF yagi antenna
  • APRS dipole antenna
  • FlightRadar24 stick antenna
  • HF magnetic loop antenna
  • 50MHz stick antenna on east-end
  • HF Multiband Dipole
  • ~90cm offset dish on a stand

G14 – Fabrics

This room is mostly fabrics, with some overflow storage for electronics.

  • Industrial sewing machine, and some domestic ones
  • Overlocker
  • Dye sublimation machine, with flat and mug heat presses
  • Embroidery machine

Storage + Server Room – G11

The storage room. Each Hacklab member has a storage box along with general storage of bigger bits of equipment that is used less frequently.

There is also a rack for members’ servers and space for Raspberry Pis.
