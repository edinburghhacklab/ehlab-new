We are repeating the very popular Soldering and Arduino workshops in the new year.

Ideal as Christmas presents, especially for those often hard-to-buy-for geeks in your family.

No previous knowledge of soldering, electronics or programming is required, we start with the basics and help you to learn at your own pace.

## Soldering Workshop

Saturday 11th January 2:30 - 5pm £18/20 \[[Information & Registration](http://edinsolder-ws.eventbrite.co.uk/)\]

## Arduino Workshop

Saturday 1st February £35/£40 (+ £20 for Arduino) \[[Information & Registration](http://edinarduino-ws.eventbrite.co.uk/)\]
