Good news everyone! After the success of the first Mini Maker Faire in Edinburgh last year, it's coming back in 2014! MMF 2014 will be held at Summerhall and promises to be bigger and better than last year.

The call for makers is open now. If you make cool things and have some interesting things to show then you could be joining the ranks of exhibitors at the Mini Maker Faire in 2014. Last year there were robots, people making things from recycled plastic, solderers, lighty up things, a tesla coil and explosions, be part of the awesome, [download the call for makers](http://edinburghhacklab.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/Call-for-makers.pdf) or check out [makerfaireedinburgh.com](http://makerfaireedinburgh.com/)!
