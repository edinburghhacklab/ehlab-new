Due to overwhelming interest the first Arduino Workshop sold out. We've scheduled a second workshop for Saturday 20th July

\[caption id="attachment\_1526" align="alignright" width="450"\][![Photo by beraldoleal http://www.flickr.com/photos/beraldoleal/6297074604 - Create Commons](images/ardunino-450x301.jpg)](http://edinburghhacklab.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/ardunino.jpg) Photo by [beraldoleal](http://www.flickr.com/photos/beraldoleal/6297074604) - Creative Commons\[/caption\]

Arduino is a micro-controller platform designed for ease of use and learning. It allows the creation of electronically controlled projects, whether it be simple blinking lights, a robot or a music generator.

This workshop is aimed at beginners. You don't need any previous electronics or programming knowledge or experience. Topics covered include:

- An introduction to the Arduino
- Using electronic components to build circuits
- Input and output
- Generating sound
- Expanding your Arduino
- And more!

Price: £45 including Arduino, £25 excluding Arduino.

All the electronic components will be provided. If you already have an Arduino you can bring that along and just purchase a kit.
